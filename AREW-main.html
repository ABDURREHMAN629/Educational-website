Our Services

We Strive to Provide the Finest Service
Possible to Our Students

AREW consultant provides A to Z services for student visa in major universities and colleges of the countries around the globe, when student come with dream of studying abroad.

1

Counselling

Applying to study abroad is a big decision, so our counseling goes beyond university selection; we guide you through applications, visas, scholarships, and more for your journey.

2

Admission Guidance

We give special attention to your application, highlighting key areas to enhance your profile, ensuring a strong impression on universities and increasing your chances of admissions.

3

University Selection

University selection is a crucial stage in the application process; it involves careful evaluation of your career goals and budget to ensure the best possible match.

4

Visa Application Assistance

We guide you through the visa process, including filling out applications, gathering required documents, and conducting mock interviews for a successful visa journey.

5

Pre- Departure Orientation

Prior to landing in your chosen country, we provide essential guidance on accommodation, culture, lifestyle, banking, and local regulations.

6

Travel Assistance

We book flights in advance, ensuring ideal departure dates, the best routes, and affordable fares so you can travel smoothly without last-minute.

7

Post-Departure Support

At AREW, we support you even after you arrive in a new country, assisting with accommodation, orientation, and any challenges to ensure a successful start.

8

Financial Aid & Scholarship Guidance

We provide guidance on securing scholarships, loans, and financial aid options to help ease your study abroad expenses and make quality education more accessible.

9

Language Test Preparation

We specialize in preparing students for IELTS, PTE, and other proficiency tests, helping them achieve their required scores with expert guidance and effective practice sessions.
